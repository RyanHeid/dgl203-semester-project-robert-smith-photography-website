About Robert Smith

A visual story teller

My name is Robert Smith. I am 32 years old and was born on the west coast in the beauriful city of Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. I started Robert Smith Photography in 2014, but I have been behind the lense of a camera since I was a teenager. Although photography was never my original plan, the idea of turning it into a career excited me to the point that it was apparent this was my call.
I've always had an innate love of photography. While growing up, I experimented with differnet art forms and mediums. Painting and drawing always let my inner artistic being run wild. I remember the first time I watched my first photograph develop right before my eyes, in the darkroom of my high school. I was immediatly fascinated and drawn into the world of photography.
Animals have always had their place in my life. From the family dog and cat, to volunteering with the local S.P.C.A, I've always been around them. When I realized that I could be around them even while at work, I jumped at the opportunity. I have had many years experience of photographing animals, and there isn't an animal that is too big, or too small for me to work with.
